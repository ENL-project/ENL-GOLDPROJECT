OUR STORY

At ENL Gold Project, we are driven by a pioneering vision - to unlock the immense mineral potential of Africa and become a leading player in the continent's Corporate mining industry. Our journey began by acquiring an exploration licenses covering a 20.1 square kilometer area in the renowned Ilesha Schist Belt of southwestern Nigeria. This Region is part of the Crystalline Basement Complex Rocks and north-south trending Upper Proterozoic meta-sedimentary and meta-volcanic belts that parallel the boundary between the West African Craton and Pan African province. The Ilesha Schist Belt is structurally divided into two major fracture zones - the Iwaraja fault to the east and Ifewara fault to the west - which are also prospective areas we are investigating.

Geology of the Tenement Area
The project area is situated in the northwestern part of Ilesa, Osun State, Nigeria. The area is generally hilly, with high relief in some localized parts (valley channels and their bounding highlands), the lowlands and valleys are mostly river channels. The highest point has an elevation of 397m; it is towards the southwestern side of the project area, while the lowest point which is towards the northern end has an elevation of 334m.


The Project area which is located at Ibokun West Ereja Local Govt of Ilesha, Osun State, Nigeria which is approximately 15.5km North West distance to Segilola Resources Operating Ltd a subsidiary of Thor Exploration Ltd.
This project area is famed for its promising gold mineralization hosted in hydrothermally altered gneiss rocks with gold-bearing quartz veins. The tenement area comprises amphibolite, amphibole schists, talc schists intimately associated with gneisses and pegmatites showing a moderately open folded style along a north-south axis.

From the onset, our team of Exploration Geologists and Geochemists immersed themselves in painstaking groundwork to evaluate the area's reserves. Preliminary assessments have identified high grade Gold Mineralization in several key areas within the Cadestral land. Initial sampling and Testing carried out by Actlabs in Ontario Canada have yielded promising Results indicating the presence of Economically viable Gold Deposits.
While artisanal miners have uncovered emplaced alluvial gold deposits, Our focus remains firmly on Primary Deposits extracting the precious minerals embedded within the schist formations that characterize this region.

With the exploration phase yielding highly positive results, ENL Gold Project is now poised to transition into full-scale mining operations.
We have cleared sites, removed overburden, established access routes, and deployed heavy machinery like bulldozers, excavators and graders to lay the groundwork. Our immediate priorities include forging technical partnerships, procuring more state-of-the-art mining and mineral processing equipment, building an expert workforce, and establishing world-class Gold testing facilities.

Underpinning our ambitions is an unwavering commitment to sustainable mining practices that balance business growth with environmental responsibility and create shared value for all stakeholders. As we embark on this exciting new phase, we remain dedicated to upholding the highest standards of ethical mining, prioritizing safety, and minimizing our environmental footprint.


Join us on this transformative journey as we unlock Africa's vast mineral wealth and propel economic development across the continent and beyond. The future is golden at ENL Gold Project.
Health & Safety

At ENL GOLD PROJECT, the health and safety of our staff, local community, and environment are our top priorities. We are committed to maintaining a safe and healthy workplace with minimal environmental impact.

Our Commitment
We follow the highest industry standards with regular safety training, proper use of personal protective equipment (PPE), and strict adherence to operational guidelines.

Annual Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA)
Each year, an EIA team evaluates our activities to ensure compliance with environmental regulations. The EIA confirms our dedication to environmental stewardship and responsible mining practices.

Health and Safety Measures
Training and Drills: Regular safety training and emergency drills keep our workforce prepared for any hazards.
Personal Protective Equipment (PPE): We provide high-quality PPE, including helmets, gloves, and goggles, to ensure on-site safety.
Safety Audits: Regular audits and inspections help us identify and address potential hazards promptly.
Health Monitoring: Routine health check-ups ensure our employees remain in good health, with medical support available as needed.

Community Engagement
Public Health Initiatives: We support local health programs to improve community well-being.
Open Communication: We maintain an open dialogue with community leaders to address concerns and keep them informed.
Support and Development: We invest in projects that enhance local infrastructure and education.

At ENL GOLD PROJECT, our commitment to health, safety, and environmental responsibility is at the core of everything we do,
ensuring lasting value for our stakeholders and a sustainable future.
