Frequently Asked Question

You May Ask

What is the ENL Gold Project?
The ENL Gold Project is ENL Consortium's venture into precious Mineral mining, specifically focused on gold mining operations.
Where is the ENL Gold Project located?
The ENL GOLD Project has an office located in Ilaje,Ilesha, Osun State, Nigeria
Who is leading the ENL Gold Project?
The ENL Gold Project is under the leadership of Mrs.A. Ajayi-OBE, who is the CEO of the ENL Mining Project.
What is ENL Consortium Ltd. experience in Corporate Mining?
ENL Consortium Ltd. is a diversified company, well established in Quarry Operations, Asphalt Productions and Gold Mining.
What stage is the ENL Gold Project currently in?
The ENL Gold Project is currently in its Exploration Stage.
Does ENL Consortium Ltd.have the necessary expertise for gold mining?
Yes, ENL Consortium Ltd. employs expert teams of professionals with experience in various core Technical areas, including Mining operations.
Is the ENL Gold Project looking for investors and partners?
Yes, ENL Consortium is actively seeking Investors and Technical partnerships for the development of their Mining operations.
What makes ENL Consortium Ltd. a suitable partner for Mineral Mining projects?
ENL Consortium Ltd. is a suitable partner for Mineral and Mining projects in Nigeria as we are fully licensed and operate under strict compliance with all rules, regulations and directives of the Federal Republic of Nigeria.
